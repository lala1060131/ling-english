ability
/əˈbɪləti/
n. 能力；才能
詳細解釋
Ability means having the skill or power to do something. It refers to a person's natural or learned capacity to perform tasks.
例句
She has the ability to solve complex problems.
她有解決複雜問題的能力。
同義詞
capability skill talent
